Market Opportunity

Why Puerto Rico, Why Now

A Caribbean-connected, bilingual market of 3.2 million consumers — a natural home for WIBISCO.

Puerto Rico is WIBISCO's most strategically significant expansion to date. The island shares food traditions, taste preferences, and household patterns common across the Caribbean — creating an immediate path for consumer acceptance.

Distribution begins mid-May 2026. The 3 weeks before shelf availability are the highest-leverage window to build an opted-in consumer audience and generate demand ahead of first purchase.

The proposed landing page — built on WordPress, Spanish-first, and mobile-optimised — serves as WIBISCO's primary digital touchpoint throughout the launch campaign.

Why This Works

Cultural affinity — Puerto Rican consumers already embrace Caribbean food heritage and island-made brands.
Bilingual market — Spanish-first page design signals genuine local investment, not a generic campaign.
High digital engagement — Puerto Rico's smartphone and social media penetration makes a digital-first approach highly effective at scale.
Diaspora connectivity — Strong Caribbean diaspora links increase the likelihood of organic word-of-mouth uptake.

Landing Page Strategy

Six Sections, One Journey

A Spanish-first WordPress landing page guiding visitors from first impression to opted-in lead.

01

Bilingual Hero

Spanish-first by default with EN toggle. Headline: "El sabor del Caribe, finalmente aquí." 85-year heritage credentials front and centre within 3 seconds.

02

Brand Explorer

Interactive cards for Shirley, Tea Times & Licias. Tap to expand flavour profiles and lifestyle context — digital shelf browsing before products arrive.

03

Email Sign-Up

Mid-page lead capture: name, email, municipality, brand preference, and opt-in consent. Powers day-one "now available near you" retail alert campaigns.

04

Flavour Explorer

Two-step mood quiz surfaces a personalised product match. Shareable result card drives organic social amplification at near-zero incremental cost.

05

Where to Buy

Retail partner list with availability status. Static list at launch; interactive store map added in Phase 2 once 5+ confirmed retail locations are verified.

06

Brand Story

"Hecho con tradición, para Puerto Rico." Emotional anchor connecting WIBISCO's Caribbean roots to Puerto Rican identity — builds trust for first-time buyers.

Tracking Stack

Analytics & Measurement

Google Tag Manager

Centralised tag management for all tracking events — brand card clicks, form submissions, quiz completions, and CTA interactions.

Meta Pixel

Retargeting audiences built from page visitors. Powers lookalike campaigns to reach new Puerto Rican consumers beyond organic reach.

Microsoft Clarity

Heatmaps and session recordings to identify friction points on the lead form and interactive sections — an ongoing optimisation tool.
