The Opportunity

Puerto Rico is WIBISCO's most strategically important expansion to date — a bilingual, Caribbean-connected market of 3.2 million consumers with deep island food traditions and high digital engagement. A focused digital-first launch, leading in Spanish and anchored by brand story, gives WIBISCO an authentic entry that feels regional, not foreign.

Distribution begins mid-May 2026. The 6–8 weeks before shelf availability are the highest-leverage window to build an opted-in consumer audience and generate demand ahead of first purchase.

Three Launch Brands

🍪 Shirley Biscuits

WIBISCO's #1 export — a golden, multi-generational icon. Positions as the everyday comfort snack that Caribbean families know by heart.

☕ Tea Times

Cream-filled sandwich biscuits in Vanilla, Chocolate & Double Chocolate. The natural match for Puerto Rico's strong café culture.

✨ Licias Crackers

Lightly Salted, Buttery Blend & Multigrain. An any-occasion snack built for lunchboxes, entertaining, and impulse purchase.

Landing Page Strategy — 6 Core Sections

01 · Bilingual Hero

Spanish-first by default with EN toggle. Headline: "El sabor del Caribe, finalmente aquí." 85-year heritage credentials front and centre.

02 · Brand Explorer

Interactive cards for Shirley, Tea Times & Licias. Tap to expand flavour profiles, tags, and lifestyle context — digital shelf browsing before products arrive.

03 · Email Sign-Up

Mid-page lead capture: name, email, municipality, brand preference, and opt-in consent. Powers the day-one "now available near you" retail alert campaign.

04 · Flavour Explorer

Two-step mood quiz surfaces a personalised product match. Shareable result card drives organic social amplification at near-zero incremental cost.

05 · Where to Buy

Retail partner list with availability status. Static list at launch; interactive store map added in Phase 2 once 5+ confirmed locations are verified.

06 · Brand Story

"Hecho con tradición, para Puerto Rico." Emotional anchor connecting WIBISCO's Caribbean roots to Puerto Rican identity. Builds trust for first-time buyers.

Tracking & Analytics Stack

Google Tag Manager

Centralised tag management for all tracking events — brand card clicks, form submissions, quiz completions, CTA interactions.

Meta Pixel

Retargeting audiences built from page visitors. Powers lookalike campaigns to reach new Puerto Rican consumers beyond organic reach.

Microsoft Clarity

Heatmaps and session recordings to identify friction points on the lead form and interactive sections. Ongoing optimisation tool.

Strategic Reminders

Spanish first — always

Default the page to ES. Engage a Puerto Rican native writer; adapted copy outperforms direct translation by a significant margin in form completion rates.

Launch the page early

Go live 6–8 weeks before mid-May distribution. The email list built in this window is the highest-value asset of the entire programme.

Store map after 5 locations

Add the interactive store finder only once 5+ verified retail partners are confirmed. An empty map erodes trust faster than no map at all.
